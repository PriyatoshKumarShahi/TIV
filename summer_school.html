Our Journey

Summer School 2023

In 2023, The Indian Vidyarthi proudly launched its first Summer School, engaging 200+ students from 17 Indian states. This pioneering initiative focused on interdisciplinary learning with workshops on policy-making, entrepreneurship, media, and technology. Students gained hands-on experience and participated in team projects mentored by top academicians and industry leaders. Many went on to receive internships and recognition for their work.

Summer School 2024

The 2024 edition expanded globally with 450+ students from over 20 countries. It introduced global masterclasses, intercultural exchange, live Q&A with international experts, and thematic learning tracks. Students built strong mentor-mentee bonds, worked on sustainability challenges, and participated in leadership simulations. It set a new benchmark for cross-border collaboration and inclusive education.

Summer School 2025

In 2025, the program is set to focus on real-world innovation, technology in governance, and digital sustainability. The upcoming edition will include capstone innovation challenges, UN SDG alignment, personalized mentor guidance, and new domains like AI in public policy and climate leadership. This year also introduces a global youth leadership council and collaborative hackathons.
